My story

I’m a developer, guitarist, and photographer currently based in Barcelona.

My photography focuses on capturing the energy and authenticity of every moment, whether through portraits, live concerts, street, or travel photography. I have a deep passion for analog photography, exploring the timeless aesthetics and textures it offers. When shooting digitally, I strive to recreate the nostalgic colors reminiscent of the golden era of photography, blending modern techniques with a love for the past.

As a developer, I specialize in backend development, primarily working with PHP and frameworks like Laravel. I’m passionate about creating scalable and efficient solutions tailored to each project’s needs. I’m also the co-founder of Liquid Studio, where we focus on delivering high-quality digital solutions.

Outside of development, I’m a guitarist, playing both electric and acoustic. I’m currently part of the band Alice & The Wonders and have had the privilege of collaborating with artists such as Chivo Chivato, Dani Flaco, Daniel Higiénico, among others.

What I create

I craft moments, whether through a camera lens, a carefully written line of code, or the strings of a guitar. My creative vision fuels my technical expertise, blending artistry and precision to create work that is both meaningful and impactful.
