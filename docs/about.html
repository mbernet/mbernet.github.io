My story

I’m a developer, guitarist, and photographer currently based in Barcelona.

As a developer, I specialize in backend development, primarily working with PHP and frameworks like Laravel. I’m passionate about creating scalable and efficient solutions tailored to each project’s needs. I’m also the co-founder of Liquid Studio, where we focus on delivering high-quality digital solutions.

Outside of development, I’m a guitarist, playing both electric and acoustic. I’m currently part of the band Alice & The Wonders and have had the privilege of collaborating with artists such as Chivo Chivato, Dani Flaco, Daniel Higiénico, among others.

I’m also a photographer, focusing on portraits for local artists, capturing live concerts, as well as street and travel photography. Through my photography, I strive to convey the energy and authenticity of each moment. My creative pursuits are a vital part of who I am and influence everything I do.

Services
